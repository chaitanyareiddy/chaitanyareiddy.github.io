01 · PROBLEM — THE DRUG WORKED. EVERY DELIVERY ROUTE FAILED.
💊
Oral
Intense pungency causes GI irritation and patient non-compliance. First-pass metabolism reduces effectiveness.
✗ PUNGENCY · NON-COMPLIANCE
🧴
Topical Cream
Stratum corneum blocks penetration. TRPV1 activation at skin surface causes burning and redness.
✗ BURNING · BARRIER BLOCKED
💉
Injection
CNS levels reach 5× blood levels. Convulsions, pulmonary oedema, bronchoconstriction. Not viable.
✗ SYSTEMIC TOXICITY · CNS
→
Insight: Dissolving microneedles (50–900µm) pierce only the stratum corneum — delivering capsaicin to subcutaneous adipose tissue. No pungency. No skin burning. No systemic exposure. No TRPV1 activation at dermal nerve endings.
