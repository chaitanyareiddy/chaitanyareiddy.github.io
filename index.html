Featured Projects

KRISPER – RAG-Based Regulatory Intelligence Co-Pilot

KRISPER is a RAG-based AI co-pilot that delivers regulatory intelligence for pharmaceutical and medical device regulatory affairs professionals. It provides compliance reports with gap analysis, 3-state trust scoring (Audit-Ready/Consult Expert/Unverified), traceable rule shelf references to FDA/EMA/ICH regulations, and isolated review sessions for FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliance. Designed to eliminate 30-40% of workflow inefficiencies in regulatory document analysis while maintaining 95% accuracy and <2% hallucination rate.

Key Features:

  • Compliance Report: Structured gap analysis before FDA/EMA submission with overall verdict, compliance score, and regulatory requirement mapping
  • 3-State Trust Score: Calibrated confidence levels (Audit-Ready 90-100%, Consult Expert 60-89%, Unverified <60%) with +350% improvement in user trust understanding
  • Rule Shelf References: Traceable citations to specific FDA/EMA/ICH/WHO regulations with authority badges and source links (+900% improvement in source traceability)
  • Isolated Sessions: Separate compliance checks per document to prevent cross-contamination, FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliant audit trails

Business Impact:

  • Annual value realization: $52,500-$78,750 per mid-market team
